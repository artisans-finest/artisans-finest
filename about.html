About

Built Around Small Batches And Good Meals

Artisan's Finest is a Virginia pantry brand from Chris & Becky, created for home cooks, grillers, hosts, and gift-givers who appreciate practical flavor made with care.

Crafted To Be Used

The goal is simple: pantry goods that look giftable, taste dependable, and earn a place near the stove instead of being saved for a special occasion.

Approachable flavorBlends are made for real meals: roasted vegetables, tacos, seafood, ribs, chicken, and weekend grilling.
Gift-ready staplesExtracts, rubs, and seasoning sets work well for host gifts, holiday baskets, and client thank-yous.
Personal serviceRequests can be matched to batch availability, gifting needs, and order size.

Meet The Makers

Two lifelong friends who turned a shared love of good food into a small-batch Virginia brand.

Co-Founder & Product Developer

Chris Marston

Chris Marston is the co-founder of Artisan's Finest and the creative force behind the company's growing collection of handcrafted seasonings, BBQ rubs, extracts, and specialty food products. Having worked in and around the food service industry since high school, Chris developed a lifelong passion for cooking, grilling, and exploring the flavors of different cultures.

His love of travel and world cuisines serves as the foundation for Artisan's Finest's diverse product line. Drawing inspiration from traditional American barbecue, Mediterranean cooking, Latin American flavors, and global culinary traditions, Chris has carefully developed and curated the seasoning and rub collections that have become a hallmark of the Artisan's Finest brand.

Together with lifelong friend Becky George, Chris transformed a shared passion for great food into a small-batch business dedicated to quality ingredients, handcrafted production, and helping customers create memorable meals at home.

Co-Founder & Extract Specialist

Becky George

Becky George is the co-founder of Artisan's Finest and the inspiration behind the company's original product line. Having spent much of her career working in kitchens and baking, Becky developed a deep appreciation for quality ingredients and the role they play in creating exceptional food.

Her passion for baking led directly to the development of Artisan's Finest's handcrafted extract collection, including the vanilla products that launched the company and continue to be among its most popular offerings. Becky's knowledge of baking, flavor development, and customer service remains central to the Artisan's Finest mission.

A lifelong friend of Chris Marston, Becky has helped build Artisan's Finest from a simple idea into a respected regional brand known for small-batch craftsmanship, quality ingredients, and personal connections with customers throughout Virginia and beyond.

What Artisan's Finest Makes

Small-batch vanilla extract, BBQ rubs, seafood seasoning, taco and fajita blends, herb-forward blends, sugar-and-salt-free options, and seasonal giftable pantry goods.
