About Artisan's Finest

About Artisan's Finest

What is Artisan's Finest?

Artisan's Finest is a small-batch producer of handcrafted seasonings, BBQ rubs, vanilla extracts, and specialty culinary products from Virginia's Shenandoah Valley. We focus on clean ingredients, bold flavor, and products made in small batches with attention to quality.

Where are you located?

We are based in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia and regularly attend festivals, farmers markets, craft shows, and food events throughout the region.

Who owns Artisan's Finest?

Artisan's Finest is owned and operated by Chris Marston and Becky George. What began as a hobby has grown into a family-run business focused on creating premium culinary products for home cooks and food enthusiasts.

Products

Products

Are your seasonings gluten-free?

Most of our seasonings are naturally gluten-free based on their ingredients. However, they are prepared in a home-based production environment that may process ingredients from multiple suppliers. Customers with severe allergies should contact us before purchasing.

Do your seasonings contain MSG?

No. Our products are crafted without added MSG.

Do your seasonings contain fillers?

No. We do not use fillers or unnecessary additives. Our focus is on herbs, spices, and ingredients chosen for flavor.

Do your seasonings contain anti-caking agents?

No. Because we do not use anti-caking agents, some natural clumping may occur. This does not affect quality or flavor.

Are your Sugar & Salt Free Seasonings truly sugar-free and salt-free?

Yes. Our Sugar & Salt Free collection contains no added sugar and no added salt, making them an excellent option for customers seeking greater control over their dietary choices.

How much seasoning is in each pouch?

Our seasoning and BBQ rub pouches contain approximately 3.5 ounces of product, or roughly ¾ cup depending on the blend.

What is the difference between a seasoning and a BBQ rub?

Seasonings are designed for everyday cooking and may be used before, during, or after cooking. BBQ rubs are specifically formulated to create flavor and bark development on grilled, smoked, or roasted meats.

Vanilla Extracts

Vanilla Extracts

How long are your vanilla extracts aged?

Our vanilla extracts are aged for a minimum of 30 weeks before release.

What types of vanilla extracts do you offer?

Current offerings may include:

  • Pure Vanilla
  • Bourbon Vanilla
  • Oak Aged Vanilla
  • Reserve Batch Vanilla
  • Golden Rum Vanilla (seasonal release)

Availability varies throughout the year.

Why are some vanilla varieties only available occasionally?

Each batch requires months of aging. Limited releases depend on aging schedules and inventory availability.

How should vanilla extract be stored?

Store tightly sealed in a cool, dark location away from direct sunlight. Properly stored vanilla extract maintains quality for years.
