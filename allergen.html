Allergen Information

Food Allergen Statement

Artisan's Finest products are handcrafted in small batches using herbs, spices, salts, sugars, vanilla beans, and other culinary ingredients.

Certain products may contain ingredients derived from the following major allergens:

  • Sesame
  • Fish (Anchovy)
  • Mustard

Our products are produced in a home-based food production environment where multiple ingredients are handled. While care is taken to avoid cross-contact, Artisan's Finest cannot guarantee that any product is completely free of allergens.

Customers with severe food allergies should review ingredient lists carefully and contact us before purchasing.

The tables below list known major allergens by product. Products marked "None Known" do not knowingly contain major allergens, but may still be produced in the shared environment described above.
