Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Artisan's Finest, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you choose to share with us.

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Any information you voluntarily provide in your message

We collect this information solely for the purpose of responding to inquiries and communicating with customers.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our website may be used to:

  • Respond to questions or requests
  • Provide information about products or events
  • Follow up regarding customer service matters
  • Improve our products and services

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses a third-party contact form service (Formspree) to process submitted inquiries. Information entered into the contact form is transmitted through that service so we can receive and respond to your message. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which, like most web hosts, may automatically log basic technical information for security and operational purposes.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by our website.

Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may use it to respond to your inquiry. We will not add you to marketing or promotional mailing lists without your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through our website. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or third-party analytics scripts. We do not build profiles of visitors or track you across other sites. If we ever introduce analytics in the future, we will update this policy.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as our Facebook page and event listings. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request information about data you have submitted
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information previously provided through our contact form

To make a request, email TheArtisansFinest@gmail.com.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out:

  • Email: TheArtisansFinest@gmail.com
  • Facebook: Artisan's Finest
  • Or use our Request Info form
