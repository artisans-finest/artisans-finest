Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

This policy explains what information Artisan's Finest collects through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Artisan's Finest ("we," "us," or "our") is a small-batch food brand based in Virginia's Shenandoah Valley, owned and operated by Chris Marston and Becky George. We respect your privacy and keep our data practices simple. This policy applies to information collected through our website at artisans-finest.github.io/artisans-finest.

Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us. Specifically, when you fill out our Request Info form, you provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The product or service you're interested in
  • Any details you include in your message (such as quantities, timing, or gift details)

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for payment information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit for one purpose: to read and respond to your request. That may include answering questions about products, checking batch availability, or following up about a gift or larger order.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information, and we do not use it to send marketing or promotional emails unless you have specifically asked us to.

How Your Information Is Handled

Our website is a set of static pages and does not run its own database. A few trusted service providers help the site function:

  • Formspree processes our Request Info form. When you submit the form, Formspree receives your entry and forwards it to us by email. Their handling of that data is governed by Formspree's own privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages hosts the website. Like most web hosts, GitHub may automatically log basic technical information (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes, governed by GitHub's privacy practices.

The messages you send us are also stored in our email account so we can reply and keep track of your request.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or third-party analytics scripts. We do not build profiles of visitors or track you across other sites.

Data Retention

We keep the requests you send only as long as needed to respond to you and to maintain a reasonable record of our correspondence. You can ask us to delete your information at any time (see below).

Your Choices and Rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information you've shared with us. To make a request, email TheArtisansFinest@gmail.com and we'll take care of it.

Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Links to Other Sites

Our site links to outside pages, such as our Facebook page and event listings. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your information, reach out:

  • Email: TheArtisansFinest@gmail.com
  • Facebook: Artisan's Finest
  • Or use our Request Info form
