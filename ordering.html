Ordering Information

How To Order

Thank you for your interest in Artisan's Finest products. At this time, orders are placed directly through us to ensure product availability and provide the best possible customer service.

  1. Step 1 — Browse Our Products

    Explore our collections:

    • Sugar & Salt Free Seasonings
    • Signature BBQ Rubs
    • Pure Vanilla Extracts
    • Heritage Sausage Seasonings
    • Specialty Bundles

    Each product page includes descriptions, ingredients, and pricing information.

  2. Step 2 — Submit a Request

    Click the Request Info button on any product page and complete the contact form. Please include:

    • Product name(s)
    • Quantity desired
    • Shipping state
    • Any questions you may have

  3. Step 3 — Receive Your Order Confirmation

    We will respond with:

    • Product availability
    • Shipping costs
    • Total purchase price
    • Payment instructions

    Most inquiries are answered within 24–48 hours.

  4. Step 4 — Complete Payment

    Payment options may include:

    • PayPal
    • Square Invoice
    • Other approved payment methods

    Instructions will be provided when your order is confirmed.

  5. Step 5 — Shipment

    Once payment is received, orders are typically packed and shipped within 3–5 business days. You will be notified when your order ships. We ship throughout the United States.

Shipping Rates

Flat-rate shipping for seasonings, BBQ rubs, and sausage blends is based on the number of items in your order:

ItemsShipping
1–2 items$5.95
3–4 items$8.95
5–8 items$12.95
9–10 items$14.95

Free shipping on orders over $100.

Vanilla extracts: Because extracts ship differently, their shipping is quoted on a per-order basis rather than at the flat rates above. Just ask for details when you place your order and we'll provide exact shipping costs with your confirmation.

Local Delivery & Event Pickup

Customers in the Shenandoah Valley may have additional options available:

  • Event pickup
  • Local delivery (when available)
  • Special arrangements by request

Ask for details when placing your order.

Questions?

We're happy to help. Use the Request Info form and we'll respond as quickly as possible.
